Our Mission

Movement that feels welcoming, accessible, and rooted in comunidad.

At Florecer Wellness, we believe movement should help people feel stronger, more confident, and more connected to themselves. Through Pilates, we create supportive spaces where people of all backgrounds, experience levels, and identities can move, grow, and feel seen.

Inspired by the meaning of florecer — “to bloom” — our goal is to help every person walk through the door feeling valued, supported, and encouraged to show up exactly as they are.

About the Founder

Creating space for people to florecer through movement.

Hi, I'm Anna, founder of Florecer Wellness. As a first-generation Latina with Cuban and Mexican roots, community has always been deeply important to me. Growing up, I often searched for spaces where I felt represented, connected, and truly welcomed. When I discovered Pilates, I found a movement practice that transformed the way I felt in my body. But I didn't always see people who looked like me in those spaces. That's why I created Florecer Wellness. My mission is to make Pilates feel warm, approachable, and community-centered while celebrating the beauty of cultura and connection. I believe movement should be for everyone, regardless of experience, background, age, or fitness level. Every class is rooted in mindful movement, genuine connection, and creating a space where people feel more at home in their bodies. Through every class, collaboration, and event, my hope is that people leave feeling stronger, more confident, and more connected to themselves and those around them. Whether you're new to Pilates or continuing your movement journey, you are always welcome here.
